Business Model and Traction

Business Model

Klen Up operates on an on-demand car wash model, providing convenience and flexibility to customers by bringing high-quality, eco-friendly cleaning services directly to their location. Our business model focuses on multiple revenue streams to ensure sustainability and growth.

Traction

Since our founding in 2023, Klen Up has successfully completed numerous car wash appointments in Riyadh, gaining positive feedback for our convenience and service quality. We are currently in the MVP Phase 1, focusing on refining our service delivery and expanding our customer base.

Our Value Proposition

Convenience

Offering car wash services that come to customers wherever they are, eliminating the need to drive to a physical location.

On-Demand Service

Customers can schedule a wash at their preferred time, making the service flexible and adaptable to busy schedules.

Revenue Streams

Pay-Per-Wash

Charge customers a set fee per wash depending on type and location.

Subscription Model

Offer monthly or weekly subscriptions for regular customers to provide discounted rates and stable revenue.

Premium Services

Additional services like waxing, interior detailing, and eco-friendly wash packages at higher price points.

Key Considerations

Partnerships

We have verbally communicated with Mrsool and Careem's decision-makers to have our app showcased on their platforms, expanding our reach and accessibility to a broader customer base.

Regulatory Environment

The Saudi Government mandates automation for car washes, resulting in non-compliant services being shut down. This regulation creates an opportunity as traffic will divert to compliant, app-based services like Klen Up.

We believe that the automotive industry will become the second biggest industry after food delivery, providing a vast market for our services.
